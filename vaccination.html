Why Vaccinate?

How Vaccines Work

Vaccines train your body to fight germs without making you sick. Like a practice drill for your immune system.

Herd Immunity

When 90% are vaccinated, even babies and elderly stay safe. We protect each other.

Save Lives & Money

Prevents costly hospital visits. One vaccine = ₹50,000 saved in treatment.

Which Vaccines & Who Should Take Them?

Children (0–15 yrs)

BCG, OPV, DPT, Measles, Hepatitis B

Pregnant Women

TT, Tdap, COVID-19

Adults (18–60)

HPV, Flu, Hepatitis A/B, Typhoid

Elderly (60+)

Pneumococcal, Flu, Shingles

Myth vs Fact

Myth: Vaccines cause the disease

Vaccines use weakened or dead germs. You cannot get sick from them.

Fact: Vaccines train immunity safely

Approved by WHO & Indian Government. Billions of doses given safely.

Myth: Too many vaccines overload immunity

Babies face 10,000+ germs daily. Vaccines are a tiny fraction.

Fact: Multiple vaccines are safe & recommended

Indian Academy of Pediatrics supports full schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are vaccines free?
Yes! All routine vaccines are 100% free at government centres across India.
Can I take vaccine during fever?
No. Wait until you recover. Mild cold is okay.
Side effects?
Mild fever or pain at injection site is normal. Give paracetamol if needed.
