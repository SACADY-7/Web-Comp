Why Balanced Diet Matters

Energy

Steady fuel all day.

Immunity

Fewer colds, infections.

Wellness

Healthy weight, mood.

What Makes a Balanced Diet

Carbs (50-55%)

Whole grains: rice, roti, millets.

Protein (10-15%)

Dal, paneer, eggs, chicken.

Fats (20-30%)

Ghee, nuts, oil (in moderation).

Budget-Friendly Tips

Seasonal Veggies

Buy local, cheaper, fresher.

Millets

Batch Cook

Myth vs Fact

Myth: Carbs are bad

Whole grains give energy.

Fact: 50-55% from carbs (ICMR)

Brown rice, roti, oats.

Myth: Need costly superfoods

Local foods work best.

Fact: Dal + rice = complete protein

NIN: Affordable & balanced.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many meals a day?
3 main + 2 snacks. Don’t skip.
Can I eat junk sometimes?
Yes, 1–2 times/week in small portions.
What are whole grains?
Brown rice, jowar, bajra, oats, whole wheat.
