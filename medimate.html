Emergency Health Tips

Stay Calm: In any emergency, remain calm to assess the situation effectively.

Hydration: Drink water every 30 minutes in heatstroke-prone areas.

Choking: Perform the Heimlich maneuver if trained, or call emergency services immediately.

Bleeding: For minor cuts, clean with water and apply a sterile bandage.

CPR: If someone faints, lay them on their back and raise their legs.

First Aid Library

Cuts & Wounds

Clean with water, apply pressure to stop bleeding, and cover with a sterile bandage.

Burns

Cool under running water for 10-15 minutes, then cover with a clean cloth.

Fainting

Lay the person on their back and elevate legs to improve blood flow.

Heatstroke

Move to a cool area, apply cool water to skin, and hydrate with water.

Heart Attack

Call emergency services immediately. Have the person sit down and rest. Administer aspirin if advised.
