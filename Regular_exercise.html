Choose Your Movement

Cardio

Running, cycling, dancing, swimming. Burns fat, boosts heart health. 150 min/week recommended.

Strength

Weights, resistance bands, bodyweight. Builds muscle, speeds metabolism. 2-3x per week.

Flexibility

Yoga, stretching, pilates. Improves mobility, reduces injury. Daily 10-15 min.

Balance

Tai chi, single-leg stands. Prevents falls, sharpens focus. Great for all ages.

Common Questions

Do I need a gym?

Nope! 80% of effective workouts use bodyweight or household items.

Home alternatives: Water bottles = weights, stairs = cardio, couch = stretching bench.

What if I hate running?

Don't run! Choose movement you enjoy:

  • Dancing (Zumba, freestyle)
  • Swimming or water aerobics
  • Cycling or stationary bike
  • Team sports (pickleball, volleyball)

Key: Consistency > intensity.

Burns 100+ calories and boosts mood instantly.
