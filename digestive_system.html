Why Digestive Health Matters

Immunity

70% of immune cells live in your gut.

Energy

Nutrients fuel your body.

Waste

Removes toxins daily.

How Digestion Works

Mouth

Chewing breaks food. Saliva starts digestion.

Stomach

Acid & enzymes turn food into liquid.

Small Intestine

Absorbs nutrients into blood.

Large Intestine

Absorbs water, forms waste.

Common Digestive Problems

Acid Reflux

Burning in chest after meals.

Constipation

Hard stools <3 times/week.

Bloating

Tight belly, gas, discomfort.

Good Habits for Digestive Health

Fiber

25g daily (dal, veggies, fruits).

Water

8 glasses. More in summer.

Move

30 min walk daily.

Sleep

7–8 hrs. Gut repairs at night.

Nutrition & Food Choices

Dal & Rice

Fiber + protein. Easy to digest.

Curd

Probiotics. Have daily.

Avoid

Oily, spicy, late-night meals.

Myth vs Fact

Myth: Only old people have bad digestion

Stress, diet affect all ages.

Fact: 1 in 3 adults has IBS

Healthline: Starts in 20s–30s.

Myth: Detox cleanses fix gut

Your liver & kidneys already detox.

Fact: Fiber & water do the job

Harvard Health: No evidence for detox.

When to Seek Help

See Doctor If

Blood in stool, weight loss, pain >2 weeks.

What to Expect

Questions, exam, maybe stool test or scan.
