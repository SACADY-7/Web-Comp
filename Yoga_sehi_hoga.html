Common Questions

Do I need to be flexible?

No! Yoga is about progress, not perfection. Start where you are — props and modifications help everyone.

Can yoga help chronic pain?

Yes! Studies show yoga reduces chronic low back pain by 56%, fibromyalgia by 30%, and arthritis symptoms significantly.

How often should I practice?

15–30 minutes daily is ideal. Even 3x/week brings major benefits. Consistency > intensity.

What if I have injuries?

Tell your teacher. Use props (blocks, straps, chairs). Restorative and chair yoga are excellent for injuries.
