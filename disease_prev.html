Why Prevent Disease?

For You

Stay active, avoid pain, live longer.

For Family

Protect children, parents, loved ones.

For Community

Stronger villages, healthier India.

Major Risks & How to Reduce Them

Poor Diet

Eat more veggies, less salt/sugar.

Inactivity

Walk 30 mins daily.

Tobacco

Quit with free helpline: 1800-11-2356

Infections

Wash hands, vaccinate, use mosquito nets.

Prevention Levels & Strategies

Primary: Avoid disease (healthy habits, vaccines)
Secondary: Early detection (BP, sugar checks)
Tertiary: Manage disease (medicines, rehab)

Healthy Habits & Environment

Diet

½ plate veggies, ¼ protein, ¼ grains.

Exercise

150 mins/week. Walk, yoga, play.

Hygiene

Wash hands, boil water, clean toilets.

Vaccines

Free at govt centers. HPV, flu, tetanus.

Common Diseases & What You Can Do

Diabetes

Check sugar yearly after 30. Walk daily.

Heart Disease

BP <140/90. Reduce salt, oil.

Dengue/Malaria

Use nets, remove stagnant water.

Myths vs Facts

Myth: Only doctors prevent disease

You control 80% with daily habits.

Fact: You are the first line of defense

WHO: Lifestyle prevents 70% of NCDs.

Myth: Vaccines aren’t needed if healthy

Vaccines prevent outbreaks.

Fact: Free at PHCs

MoHFW: 12 crore vaccinated in 2024.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is screening?
Simple tests (BP, sugar) to catch disease early. Free at PHCs.
Can I afford prevention?
Yes! Walk, eat home food, use govt services.
When to see a doctor?
Persistent cough, weight loss, high BP, chest pain.
