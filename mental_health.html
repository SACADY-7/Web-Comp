Why Mental Health Matters

Daily Life

Better focus, energy, joy.

Work

Less burnout, more productivity.

Relationships

Stronger bonds, less conflict.

Common Issues & Signs

Anxiety

Constant worry, racing heart, restlessness.

Depression

Sadness >2 weeks, no interest in life.

Stress/Burnout

Exhaustion, irritability, poor sleep.

What You Can Do: Self-Care
