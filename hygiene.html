Why Move & Stretch?

Physical Health

Better flexibility, stronger bones, weight control. Reduces diabetes risk by 30% (ICMR).

Mental Wellness

40% less stress, better sleep, improved mood (Harvard). Yoga calms the mind.

For Every Indian

1 in 4 adults is sedentary. Start at home — safe, proven, free.

Yoga & Exercise Types

Hatha Yoga

Slow, gentle poses. Perfect for beginners and seniors.

Walking/Cardio

Brisk walk, stairs. Great for heart health.

Bodyweight Strength

Squats, push-ups. Build muscle at home.

Gentle/Restorative

Supported poses for stress and sleep.

Getting Started Safely

Consult Doctor

If pregnant, heart issues, or recent surgery.

What You Need

Comfortable clothes, mat (or towel), 6x6 ft space.

Modifications

Use chair for balance, wall for support.

Source: Indian Yoga Association, ACSM
